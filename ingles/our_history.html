Our history


Our story began in 2020, of simple projects and technological activities promoted at Etec that made it possible to awaken “being an entrepreneur” in a young group of 6 members united by a purpose: The recognition in the outside market for the work offered in the software industry and which values quality, B2B / B2C partnership and excellence in its service.

It was an arduous trajectory for the company, where we had moments when we thought that everything would end and without being able to continue with the company, competing in such a competitive market that we face. But the union, the strength, the drive and the love for our work made us reflect to move forward and thus go on in the struggle for the growth of the company to provide the best to our customers.
